Welcome to the Mendota Yahara Watershed Historical Forest Patches Viewer!

This tool can be used to explore changes in forest patches in the watershed over time. Historical imagery can be changed in the upper right hand corner of the map.

While this application version only displays the most recent decade of data, imagery going back to 1937 was analyzed revealing about a 0.7% decrease in forest area over the entire watershed.
